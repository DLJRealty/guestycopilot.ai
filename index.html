FAQ
Common questions
Does it work with Airbnb, VRBO, and Booking.com?
Yes. It works with every channel Guesty supports — including Airbnb, VRBO, Booking.com, and your direct booking website. If Guesty gets the message, our AI handles it.
Can I review messages before they're sent to guests?
Absolutely. With the Smart Responder plan, you review and approve every AI draft before it sends. With Autonomous Host, the AI sends instantly but you can switch to review mode anytime. You're always in control.
What happens with emergencies or complex issues?
Our AI has smart escalation built in. Maintenance emergencies, guest complaints, safety issues, and anything it's not confident about gets routed to you immediately via your preferred notification method. The AI acknowledges the guest and lets them know a human is on it.
How long does setup take?
Under 10 minutes. Connect your Guesty account, and our AI automatically pulls your property details, house rules, and past conversations. It starts learning your hosting style immediately and can go live the same day.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel with one click. If you're a beta customer with the 50% lifetime discount, your discount stays locked in as long as you remain subscribed.
Is my Guesty data secure?
Your API credentials are encrypted with AES-256 at rest and never exposed. Each customer's data is completely isolated. We never share or sell your data. Your guest conversations stay between you and your guests.
What IoT devices does the Enterprise tier support?
The Enterprise IoT tier currently supports Tuya, Google Nest, and Samsung SmartThings ecosystems. This covers smart locks, thermostats, noise monitors, water leak sensors, and more. We're continuously adding new integrations based on customer demand.
